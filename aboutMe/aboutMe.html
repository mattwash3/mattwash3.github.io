About Matt Washington





I'm a former Technical Engineer Paratrooper in the US Army. I run a part time hobby business called Samurai Sewing making custom military gear and 3d printing figures and parts. I'm a creator, an artist and a craftsman who loves learning new skills and expanding on current skills. I'm mostly self taught and enjoy doing research on new things in my free time. One thing I find very exciting about technology and crafting is the near limitless possibilites that are usually only limited by your creativity! Every new day brings a new opportunity to be able to learn something new or imrove yourself!
